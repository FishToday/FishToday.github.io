The origins of the essential fishing app

FishToday is our solution to the lack of support for the fishing community. There historically hasn't been a website or app designed for the fisher but FishToday.ca is here to change that and revolutionize the industry. FishTody.ca's intuitive user centred design allows users to find fishing buddies, find amazing deals on the latest fishing gear and plan for the fishing trip of a lifetime all in the convenience of one simple but powerful website and app. It places all this information in the fingertips of the fisher in one convenient package and we're continually looking to improve our service.

Isn't FishToday awesome? If you're not convinced yet, listen to this; it syncs across the cloud. Yes, your data is securely synced through all your devices so you can access your profile anywhere at any time. Start planning your trip on your desktop and send out the invitations from your phone.
